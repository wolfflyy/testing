HCmute University

Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education is currently listed as one of the top 10 universities in Vietnam and also a member in the top group of Southeast Asia universities. This is a public university located in Thủ Đức City, about 10 km north-east from downtown Ho Chi Minh City

Saigon Technology University

Saigon Technology University is a university in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. It was established from the Ho Chi Minh City Technology College, founded on 24 September 1997 by Decision number 198/QĐ-TTg signed by the prime minister of Vietnam.
